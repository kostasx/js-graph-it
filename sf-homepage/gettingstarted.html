Getting started

A sample page using js-graph-it

To guide you through the basic concepts of js-graph-it, we will build a sample page step by step describing every addition to the code.

During this very quick tutorial, use the "previous" and "next" buttons at the bottom of the page to move forward and backward through the steps.

You can also "copy-and-paste" the code into a file and test the partial results yourself. Just pay a little attention to where you put the files: the best thing to do is to create a folder somewhere on your pc and copy the js-graph-it.js and js-graph-it.css files in it, then create the sample files into the same directory.
